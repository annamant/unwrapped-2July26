Help us pilot something for local shops

We're running a small pilot for independent shops, cafés, studios and makers. It's called Unwrapped, and it is built by local people who want neighbourhoods to do more than find businesses on a map.

Unwrapped gives businesses a way to tell nearby people when something is happening — a new product, a discount, a deal, a launch or a one-off. You post a short drop; nearby shoppers get an alert and can claim a QR ticket. It isn't for listing your whole menu or catalogue — it's for the moments worth sharing.

We're testing this with a small group first, and we don't have it all figured out yet. We want your feedback: what's useful, what's missing, and what would make you want to post a drop. If you join, you're helping shape what it becomes.
